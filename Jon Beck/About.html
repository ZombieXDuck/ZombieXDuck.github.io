ABOUT JON BECK

Jon Beck has been working in the Photography industry for over 15years.
Jon is a professional photographer who combines precise technical skills
with an approachable and personable demeanour to help you achieve your vision. Jon's pictures are not
souvenirs, but images that help us to ‘see’ and to understand landscapes and people as art.
Light literally exudes from Jon’s pictures like few others, and this luminescent enlightenment
startles us with its clarity and perception With a wide knowledge of photographic skills, Jon photographs
many subjects beyond his portfolio that is seen online. You can keep up to
date with David's latest projects through Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
